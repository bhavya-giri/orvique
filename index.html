Core Features

Every detail, considered.

Smart Radar Auto Lid

Infrared proximity sensing detects your approach within 1.2 m. The cushioned lid lifts silently in 0.3 s — no touch required. Closes automatically once you leave.

Heated Seat

Multi-level ceramic heating maintains the seat at 38 °C, 40 °C, 42 °C or 45 °C — activated the moment you approach, always ready at the right temperature.

Instant Water Heating

Tank-free heating technology delivers warm water at precisely 30–40 °C the instant you need it — no waiting, no cold surprise, no energy wasted keeping a tank hot.

LED Status Display

A bright LED panel shows real-time seat temperature, water warmth, and wash mode at a glance — paired with the soft-close lid for a complete premium experience.

SIAA Antibacterial Seat

Ag⁺ silver-ion technology embedded in the seat material delivers a 99.99 % antibacterial rate, certified by SIAA — stays hygienic between every use without any spray or wipe.

IPX4 Waterproof

Fully splash-proof electronics and sealed components rated to IPX4 — the entire unit is safe in the humid bathroom environment, from daily splashes to full cleaning rinses.

Smart Display & Control

The right temperature, always.

An illuminated display ring shows your seat and water temperature at a glance, while the wireless remote gives you precise control over every setting — wash mode, pressure, warmth — from anywhere in the room.

  • Real-time seat temperature readout
  • Wireless remote with compact design
  • One-touch knob for quick adjustments
  • Night light for comfortable use after dark

4 Wash Modes

Personalised hygiene, every time.

Four precision-tuned wash modes adapt to your needs — whether a gentle rinse, full rear cleanse, oscillating massage or targeted feminine care, the right mode is one tap away.

  • Gentle Feminine — soft, targeted spray
  • Rear Wash — wide-angle full-coverage rinse
  • Oscillating — back-and-forth massage motion
  • Bowel Assist — gentle pulsating stimulation

Every Detail Considered

Thoughtful by design.

Warm Air Drying

Multi-level warm air drying at room temp, 40 °C, 48 °C, or 55 °C — a complete dry-after-wash experience, gentle and moisture-free.

One-Touch Knob & Night Light

Smart Wireless Remote
