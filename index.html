Core Features

Every detail, considered.

Auto Open & Close

Proximity sensors detect your approach and the lid lifts silently in 0.3 s — no touch, no buttons. It closes automatically once you leave.

Heated Seat

Ceramic heating elements maintain the seat between 32 °C and 40 °C, with four precision steps and instant-ready activation on approach.

Vortex Flush

Dual-inlet swirl nozzles generate a 360° vortex that cleans the bowl completely using 18 % less water than conventional flush systems.

HD LCD Panel

A 6.8 cm colour display shows water temperature, seat warmth and mode settings at a glance — everything readable without bending down.

Intelligence, visible

HD LCD Control Panel

Mounted flush to the ceramic body, the display renders settings in sharp colour. Adjust wash temperature, seat warmth and cleansing mode without reaching for the remote.

  • 6.8 cm, 1280 × 720 colour LCD
  • Auto-brightness adapts to ambient light
  • Multilingual interface
  • Full wireless handset included

Vortex Flush

360° rinse, every time.

Twin inlets at opposite sides of the rim produce a powerful swirl that reaches every surface. No brush required — the bowl self-cleans with each flush.

  • 18 % less water than standard flush
  • Full 360° bowl coverage
  • Stain-resistant nano glaze
  • Fully rimless inner bowl
